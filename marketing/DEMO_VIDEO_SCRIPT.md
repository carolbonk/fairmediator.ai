# FairMediator Demo Video Script (2 Minutes)

**Recording Tool:** Loom (loom.com)
**Target Length:** 1:45-2:00 minutes
**Tone:** Professional, confident, data-driven
**Format:** Screen recording with webcam bubble

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Clean browser (close extra tabs, use Incognito mode)
- [ ] Prepare test data: "Acme Corp" and "BigLaw LLC" as opposing parties
- [ ] Have 3 mediator names ready to demonstrate (use real seed data)
- [ ] Test audio (clear, no background noise)
- [ ] Rehearse once (time yourself, should be under 2 minutes)

---

## Script

### [0:00-0:15] Hook + Problem (15 seconds)

**[CAMERA ON, PAUSE, SMILE]**

"Hi, I'm [Your Name]. I'm going to show you how to find conflicts of interest that mediators don't disclose—in under 30 seconds."

**[PAUSE]**

"40% of mediators have conflicts. Most lawyers never find them because manual searches take hours."

**[TRANSITION TO SCREEN SHARE]**

---

### [0:15-0:40] Demo: Single Conflict Check (25 seconds)

**[SCREEN: FairMediator Home Page]**

"Here's how FairMediator works."

**[TYPE: Opposing parties "Acme Corp" and "BigLaw LLC"]**

"First, I enter the opposing parties and law firms in my case."

**[CLICK: Start Conflict Check]**

"Then I search for a mediator."

**[TYPE: Mediator name, e.g., "Kenneth Feinberg"]**

**[RESULTS APPEAR - SHOW RED/YELLOW/GREEN BADGE]**

"In 30 seconds, I get a risk score."

**[CLICK: View Details]**

"This mediator has a RED flag—he received $15,000 in donations from opposing counsel's firm and lobbied for their client."

**[PAUSE for emphasis]**

"I would never have found this manually."

---

### [0:40-1:10] Demo: Batch Checking (30 seconds)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Batch Conflict Checker]**

"Now, let's say I have a list of 20 mediators to vet."

**[CLICK: Upload CSV]**

"I upload a CSV file with all their names."

**[UPLOAD FILE - SHOW PROGRESS BAR]**

"FairMediator checks all 20 in under 2 minutes."

**[RESULTS TABLE APPEARS]**

"Here are the results:"

**[POINT TO STATS]**

"8 mediators are clear. 7 need review. And 5 have serious conflicts."

**[CLICK: Export Results]**

"I can export this to CSV and share it with my team."

---

"And LinkedIn connections—50 mutual connections with opposing counsel."

**[PAUSE]**

"All in one place. No manual research needed."

"Pricing is simple:"

**[POINT TO FREE TIER]**

"3 free conflict checks to start. No credit card required."

"Stop guessing about mediator neutrality."
